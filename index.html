Keenan Powers

Welcome to my world! I'm a passionate individual with a zest for innovation and a mission to make a positive impact.

Skills

With a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and a minor in Computer Science under my belt (graduating in May 2023) and a Master of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering on the horizon (May 2024), I'm well-equipped for a dynamic career in product management and technical sales. My strengths lie in communication, strategic thinking, and a drive for innovation: qualities I'm eager to apply to help people in various capacities. Whether it's finding solutions, guiding teams, or supporting others, I make a difference in the communities I am a part of.

Hobbies

Beyond my work, I'm creative at heart. I am an avid team sports enthusiast, valuing the camaraderie and teamwork they bring. My favorite sports to play are basketball, soccer, spikeball, and ultimate frisbee. I love to cook for myself and others; I am especially proud of my homemade kefir and the hot sauce recipes, made with love, that I continually develop and improve. I also bake casseroles and other home-cooked meals for Urban Ministries to feed families in the triangle and have donated blood every 56 days since 2021. I enjoy designing personalized knickknacks (see Designs), including stickers and coasters. Reach out to me for custom designs!

Community

My commitment extends to social causes, including my role as the Social Chair of the oSTEM@Duke chapter, where we foster inclusivity in STEM fields. I'm also deeply invested in education. Since 2019, I have been a teaching assistant for more than 20 classes. Additionally, I am a substitute teacher for Raleigh Charter High School. I primarily sub for math and science courses, but I have done English and history as well. Join me on this journey, where we can innovate, connect, and work together to create positive change.
